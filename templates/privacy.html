Privacy Policy

I take your privacy seriously. Stratify doesn’t collect or store any personal data. Everything you type stays on your device unless you explicitly export a file or share something.

Local Storage

Stratify may use your browser's local storage to save planner inputs during your session, but nothing is ever sent to a server.

Third-Party APIs

Stratify uses the OpenAI API to generate planning content. Only the text you submit is sent to OpenAI, and that data is not stored or used to train their models according to their API data policy.

No Tracking or Ads

There are no trackers, cookies, ads, or analytics scripts running on Stratify. Just planning tools—nothing else.

Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to email me directly at stratify(at)fastmail.com.
